who am i?

I’m a multidisciplinary creative with a background in textile design and a growing practice in graphic design, video editing, and creative direction. A recent graduate from NIFT New Delhi, I approach visual storytelling with both a design foundation and a strong sense of narrative, blending craft with contemporary content.

In early 2025, I completed a four-month graduation project with Jaypore – ABFRL, where I designed two menswear collections for SS26 rooted in traditional block printing techniques. Beyond design, I also contributed to the shoot direction and photo editing, deepening my interest in visual content creation.

I’m especially passionate about video—from ideation and shooting to editing—and enjoy working on projects that explore brand identity and digital storytelling. As a fresher, I’m committed to creating visuals that are clear, consistent, and engaging, while continuously learning through hands-on experience.
